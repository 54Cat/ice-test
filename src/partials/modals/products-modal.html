Ice cream

  • Subtitle

    Ice cream is a mixture of milk, cream, sugar, and sometimes other ingredients that has been frozen into a soft, creamy delight using special techniques.

  • Subtitle

    Ice cream has been a popular treat for hundreds of years but has only become commonplace since the widespread use of refrigeration.

  • Subtitle

    A rich, sweet, creamy frozen food made from variously flavored cream and milk products churned or stirred to a smooth consistency during the freezing process and often containing gelatin, eggs, fruits, nuts, etc.
